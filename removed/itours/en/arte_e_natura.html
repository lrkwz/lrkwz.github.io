Art and Nature combined

Parks and gardens devoted to contemporary art.

A look at the twentieth century: the Museo Pagani at Castellanza (Va)

A sculpure park where the great sculptors of the twentieth century can be seen side by side:
from Jean Arp to Fernand Léger, from Man Ray to Arnaldo Pomodoro

Found on the spot: Arte Sella at Borgo Valsugana (Tn)

A 3 kilometre path in the woods where pieces of sculpture made from material found on the spot are scattered. The climax is a gothic cathedral made from trees and intertwined branches

Inside and out: Marrana di Ameglia (Sp)

A hill overlooking the sea scattered with works created by great international artists so as to interrelate with the surroundings

A little girl's dream: the Giant's garden at Cento (Fe)

An enchanted place, which is the result of a little girl's imagination combined with the craftsmanship of her father, the sculptor Marco Pellizzola. A world of giant battlements and roofless castles.

Beings in the woods: a sculpture park at Chianti di Castelnuovo (Si)

Scattered through the woods are twenty-five works created on the spot to invite people to meditate on the relationship between Man and Nature

A city of the imagination: the Tarot Gardens at Capalbio (Gr)

This is a sort of art Disneyland where you walk among enormous sculptures sparkling with light and colour, which connect fairy-tale, nightmare and playing-cards

Treasure Hunting: the Spoerri Gardens at Seggiano (Gr)

A hillside scattered with ironical, irrespectful, fascinating, hidden works of art.A treasure hunt to discover genial and gifted artists from all over the world

Antique mysteries: the Park of Monsters at Bomarzo (Vt)

Huge statues and monstrous creatures created “ only to relieve the heart” by Prince Orsini in 1500. An open air museum of manieristic art

A Pleasure Garden: the Medici Park at Pratolina di Vaglia (Fi)

A place of 16th century wonder created by the architect Buontalenti where Nature and water are blended, to our amazement, into a magnificent garden, overseen by the great Colossus of the Appenines

Art in Nature: Art- in- a- Wood Museum at Calcata (Vt)

Living art, created from materials found in woods, as natural as possible. Works which blend in and change with their surroundings
