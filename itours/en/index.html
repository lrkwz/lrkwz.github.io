Who we are

We are a group of communication professionals who share a love for our country. We are interested in villages, sites, historical towns, offtrack places, landscape, genuine cuisine. We have decided to pull our considerable journalistic, technological and IT competence in order to allow as many people as possible to share and enjoy the significant experiences over years of travelling in Italy.

iTours.it: discovering Italy through a multi-medial journey

Accompanied by our audioguides you will discover the most fascinating cultural, artistic and natural spots in Italy.

Our voices will take you along routes, indicating the most interesting views or the most intriguing details. We will also select appetizing places where you can eat; there will be a series of short itineraries described in detail and which will help you to appreciate famous and less famous locations in our country.

iTours can show you:

  • the most interesting tourist routes in less well known areas of Italy
  • the most intriguing parks and open-air contemporary art exhibitions
  • food and drink tours to taste the best regional cousines
  • unusual DOC wines which have an interesting history ... and a special taste
  • the most beautiful mountain landscapes to enjoy while walking
  • proven bike routes in the midst of nature
